Recent works

The selected works are the result of my activity during the last 10 months or so. It's a mix between projects and experiments (lab), and their level of achievement is not equal; however I found it interesting to expose them as a sample of what I can produce and what drives me.

01 Spectrum analyser
01 Spectrum analyser

(2021) Spectrum analyser is a project realized during a 5 days workshop based on the canvas and the web-audio API. My goal was to capture the user's voice and words thanks to the web-speech API and create a real time reproduction of his spectrogram via Three.js.

02 La formidable aventure d'Orsay
02 La formidable aventure d'Orsay

(2021) MVP project made during a workshop in collaboration with the Orsay Museum. We wanted to create an immersive experience designed for children to discover the "Art nouveau" through the completion of micro-quests. The objective was to establish the experience as the beginning of a game that could be used in all sections of the museum.

03 Metamorph
03 Metamorph

(2022) Metamorph is a small personal lab project that I realized in order to improve myself with Three.js and the creation of custom shaders.
